The 5panel.dance Deployable Thing Node

Hi there! You've either discovered my node out in the wild, or you've stumbled upon this page digging through my site's source. Either way, you might be wondering what this thing you just found is!

Meshtastic is an open source, long-range, low cost, off-grid communications protocol. The node you found here is a sort of solar-powered "repeater" that receives messages and signals, and sends them back out again. It helps enable long distance communication without the need for Wi-Fi, cell service, or any other infrastructure. Cool, right?

Questions

  • Q: Is this node dangerous?
  • A: Nope! It's just a solar panel, cheap radio, battery, and an antenna, in a neat little box. The battery has appropriate over/undercharge protection circuitry built in.
  • Q: Does this node collect any information from nearby devices? Is it a privacy concern?
  • A: Not at all! This device can only accept a Bluetooth connection from my phone, and cannot see or monitor other devices, except for other nearby Meshtastic nodes. If you don't carry a Meshtastic node, it won't even know you're there!
  • Q: Is it worth stealing?
  • A: Probably not. The full bill of materials is like $30 tops. It wouldn't really turn any profit.
